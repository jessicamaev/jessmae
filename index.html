The Arty Crowd

A digital hub focused on increasing access to and discovery of Hamilton's art scene for artist, arts organizations and arts lovers. As the Chair of The Arty Crowd Steering Committee, I served as a pivotal liaison between the Hamilton Arts Council and the Web Development team, collaborated with The Safer Spaces Project on refining self-identification forms, supervised a HWDSB co-op student as The Arty Crowd Youth Representative, produced accessible how-to videos and crafted a toolkit for initiating digital hubs in Arts Organizations.
